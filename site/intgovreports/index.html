    2024 Reports¶

    Kenya Youth IGF | November 2024¶

    Location: Nairobi, Kenya
    Organizer: Kenya Youth IGF Team
    Participants: ~150

    The inaugural Kenya Youth IGF brought together young people from across the country to discuss digital rights, data governance, and youth participation in policy processes. Key recommendations on youth data protection were submitted to the national IGF multistakeholder group.

    Documents: - Outcome Document - Full Report - Policy Recommendations

    Asia-Pacific Youth IGF | August 2024¶

    Location: Bangkok, Thailand
    Organizer: APrIGF Youth Initiative
    Participants: ~200

    Regional gathering focused on AI governance, digital skills development, and cross-border data flows. Produced a youth statement delivered at the main APrIGF event.

    Documents: - Youth Statement - Session Reports

    2023 Reports¶

    Ghana National IGF | October 2023¶

    Location: Accra, Ghana
    Organizer: Ghana IGF Secretariat
    Participants: ~300

    Multi-stakeholder event addressing cybersecurity, digital identity, and e-governance. Youth track included 50+ participants who contributed recommendations on data protection framework revisions.

    Documents: - Outcome Document - Youth Track Report

    European Youth IGF | June 2023¶

    Location: Brussels, Belgium
    Organizer: EuroDIG Youth Programme
    Participants: ~100

    Youth-led discussions on platform regulation, content moderation, and digital rights in the context of the Digital Services Act and AI Act.

    Documents: - Youth Messages - Full Report
